# Our Services

We design, build, and operate backend systems, web applications, and the infrastructure that runs them. Our default stack is Go and JavaScript on Linux, fronted and protected by Cloudflare — picked because each piece is simple, well-understood, and easy to operate at a small scale.

## Custom Software Development

We build bespoke software when off-the-shelf products are the wrong fit. Most of our work is server-side: services and APIs written in **Go (Golang)** for throughput and predictable performance, with **JavaScript/TypeScript** front-ends where the problem calls for an interactive UI. Every system we ship is designed to be observable and straightforward to operate.

## Application Development & Maintenance

Beyond greenfield builds, we maintain and evolve existing applications. That includes fixing legacy bugs, refactoring brittle code, modernizing runtimes, and adding features incrementally without destabilizing what already works. We treat maintenance as a first-class activity, not an afterthought.

## Infrastructure & DevOps

We design and run the Linux infrastructure underneath our software: containerized workloads, CI/CD pipelines, TLS and DNS, backups, and monitoring. We make heavy use of **Cloudflare** for authoritative DNS, edge caching, and zero-trust access, which lets small teams operate like much larger ones.

## IT Consultancy

Sometimes the right deliverable is advice, not code. We take on short engagements to review architecture, audit security posture, untangle legacy systems, or help an internal team get unblocked. Engagements are scoped tightly and end with concrete, written recommendations.

## How We Work

We prefer small teams, short feedback loops, and written specifications before implementation. Estimates are honest; if a problem is genuinely hard, we say so. We communicate primarily in English over email and shared documents, and we keep clients close to the work — no black-box development.
